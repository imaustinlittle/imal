The Homelab Rack

The central nervous system of my digital life. A 42U enterprise-grade setup focusing on redundancy, compute power, and AI experimentation.

Infrastructure Breakdown

Compute Node 1: Dell R730

The media powerhouse.

  • OS: Unraid
  • Role: Plex Media Server & Management
  • Acceleration: 2x Nvidia Tesla P40 (24GB VRAM each)
  • Use Case: Large Language Models (LLMs) & AI Projects

Compute Node 2: Dell R730XD

Storage and general purpose compute.

  • OS: Unraid
  • Role: Network Video Recorder (NVR)
  • Containerization: Docker Host (Experimental/General)
  • Storage: High-density 12-bay configuration

AI Workstation Node

Custom-built high-performance rig.

  • OS: Ubuntu Server
  • GPU: Nvidia Quadro RTX 5000
  • Primary Task: Hardware Transcoding
  • Secondary Task: AI Model Training & Experimentation

Networking & Core

The backbone of the operation.

  • Rack: 42U Dell Enterprise Rack
  • Switching: TP-Link Omada SDN
  • Routing: OPNsense (Virtualize/Physical)
